Principles

The principles of the Church of Azerate are simple. We are law-abiding and we do not advocate for the unwarranted harm or killing of others, human or otherwise. We believe in the sanctity of life and that all people are created equal.

We value almost above all else, the fufillment of our true will and purpose in life. To follow Azerate is to follow the path of unfettered enlighenment, desire, and personal knowledge. Regardless of societal norms and expectations.
